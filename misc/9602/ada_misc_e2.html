Term Life Insurance premiums increase annually based on age. Disability Income Protection Insurance premiums increase every five years based on age. Office Overhead Expense Disability Insurance premiums increase every 10 years based on age to age 60 and every 5 years thereafter to age 75. Premium credits are not guaranteed and reevaluated periodically. Coverage for dental student members is issued regardless of condition if under age 60, but benefits are not payable for disabilities resulting from a sickness, disorder, physical condition or symptom that existed or was treated within 6 months prior to enrollment until guaranteed issue coverage has been in effect for at least 12 consecutive months. Coverage automatically renews each academic year. Individuals may convert coverage to the plans for practicing dentists after graduation by paying member premiums and maintaining continuous ADA membership.
